Qualmia OÜ — Technology Validation Lab

Qualmia OÜ is an Estonian venture studio and applied innovation laboratory focused on building and validating digital product hypotheses based on emerging technologies. We experiment, build MVPs, test them in real markets, and graduate the ideas that prove themselves.

Live Projects & Current Efforts

We are actively developing and commercializing early-stage concepts:

GA-Assistant — AI Analytics Interface

Qualmia is building the GA-Assistant, a ChatGPT App that allows users to connect their Google Analytics account and discuss website performance in natural language. Instead of dashboards, queries like "How did my traffic change?" or "What drives conversions?" become conversations. The app interprets analytics data, provides business insights, and helps users make better commercial decisions.

This project is currently in product incubation and moving toward its first validation stage. Contributors joining this initiative enter the process as credited builders, not unpaid helpers.

Core Capabilities

We build MVPs to test feasibility and demand. A minimal viable product (MVP) is the simplest functional version of a product that can validate real user or commercial interest. Our development focus includes AI-native analytics, secure communications, IoT-integrated digital infrastructure, blockchain-based product models, and LLM-visibility engineering.

The Qualmia Venture Flow

Product incubation follows a disciplined process: structured brainstorming, product hypothesis, MVP specification, development, and market validation. When validation signals real demand, the project graduates into an independent startup entity, ready for funding and commercial launch.

Fairness-First Equity Model

Early contributors join projects voluntarily, logging credited work hours that later convert into proportional equity — but only if the idea passes validation and graduates into its own project company. Ownership is earned through measurable contribution, not negotiation or politics.

If validation fails, no one loses anything except the adventure.
