GA Assistant — AI Analytics Interface

Turn GA4 data into decisions

GA Assistant is a ChatGPT App that allows users to connect their Google Analytics account and discuss website performance in natural language. Instead of dashboards, queries like "How did my traffic change?" or "What drives conversions?" become conversations. The app interprets analytics data, provides business insights, and helps users make better commercial decisions.

Example Questions

Ask like a human: "Where are visitors coming from?" "Which channels work best?" "Why is conversion low?"

FAQ

Do you store my GA4 data?

By default, the app should only access what's needed to answer your question. Implementation details may evolve; see the Privacy Policy for the current approach.

What access do you need?

Read-only access to your GA4 property (so the assistant can query metrics and dimensions).

Is this an official Google product?

No. GA Assistant is an independent tool built to work with Google Analytics 4.

This project is currently in product incubation and moving toward its first validation stage. Contributors joining this initiative enter the process as credited builders, not unpaid helpers.
